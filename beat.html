Beatrice Ng'ethe

  1. ADVOCATE
  2. OF HIGH COURT
Bachelor of Laws Degree (LL.B)

Beatrice is an advocate of the High Court of Kenya, a Practicing Certified Company Secretary and a Certified Governance Auditor at Imperial Registrars. She is a practicing member of the Institute of Certified Public Secretaries of Kenya, with over five (5) years post qualification experience. She also is a Certified Public Accountant and holds a Law Degree from University of Nairobi. She has immense experience and wide-ranging knowledge and experience in Company Secretarial Practice, corporate governance, Company Law and practice, maintenance of statutory records, and is well versed with the processes of the office of the Registrar of Companies and of societies. She also has vast knowledge in Conveyancing, Commercial, Criminal, Corporate, Family and succession and Intellectual Property Law among other areas of the Law. She has vast in experience in governance, commercial, company, labor and immigration law and she is in charge of various re-current company secretarial work and providing professional legal advice and consultancy to a diverse portfolio of clients in the following sectors; Trading and Commercial Organizations, financial institutions, non-governmental organizations, building and construction, telecommunications, agro- based service industry.
