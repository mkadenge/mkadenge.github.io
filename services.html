Company Secretarial Services.

The secretarial team has over the years offered its vast array of clients with exemplary secretarial services by meeting the statutory compliance requirements under the Companies Act 2015 and other relevant laws.

Routine Secretarial Services.
Secretarial Audits.
Filing of annual secretarial returns.
Acquisition and renewal of business permits & licenses.
