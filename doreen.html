Doreen Alivitsa

Director

Doreen is the business development executive and a Company Secretary at Imperial Registrars. She holds a bachelor’s degree in Community development from Daystar University. She spearheads training and runs both the International Clientele and Customer Relationship Departments in Imperial Registrars. She has over (5) years post qualification experience and is well versed in Corporate Advisory, Company formations, re-organizations, convening and moderating AGMs, maintaining records of various meetings; maintenance of statutory records, compliance and de-registration of companies. She is also involved in Board and Members’ Governance training, board induction and evaluation; laws and procedures of meetings. She also has vast experience in Immigration Services- Acquisition of long term and short term work permits, Visas, Passes and Alien Cards, Trade Licensing Acquisition/ Renewal of trade licenses and permits, Eg NCA, AGPO, YAGPO, Single Business Permit.
